WatchCTRL Mac

Control your Mac from your Apple Watch.
Anki flashcards, scrolling, media — all from your wrist.

Download for Mac

Requires macOS 14.0 or later • Apple Silicon & Intel

How it works

  • Install the WatchCTRL iOS app on your iPhone (available on the App Store)
  • The iPhone relays commands from your Apple Watch to this Mac app
  • Use your watch to control Anki reviews, scroll pages, play/pause media, and more
  • iPhone and Mac must be on the same Wi-Fi network

Setup

  1. Open the DMG and drag WatchCTRL Mac to Applications
  2. Launch the app — it lives in your menu bar (no dock icon)
  3. Grant Accessibility permission when prompted
    (System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility)
  4. Open the WatchCTRL app on your iPhone — it will auto-connect
  5. Use your Apple Watch buttons to control your Mac
