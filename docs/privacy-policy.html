Privacy Policy — WatchCTRL

Last updated: 21 February 2026

Overview

WatchCTRL is a gesture-based remote control app for iPhone and Apple Watch. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data — ever.

Data We Process

Motion & Gesture Data

Your Apple Watch uses its motion sensors to detect hand gestures. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored or sent anywhere outside of your personal device ecosystem. It is not retained after the gesture is acted upon.

Local Network

WatchCTRL may use your local Wi-Fi network to relay commands to other devices you own. No data is transmitted over the internet or to any third party.

Bluetooth

WatchCTRL uses Bluetooth to communicate between your iPhone and other devices you own. No Bluetooth data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

In-App Browser

WatchCTRL includes a built-in browser. All browsing activity, bookmarks, and recently visited URLs are processed and stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your browsing activity. No browsing data is collected, uploaded, or shared with any party, including us.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or sign-in
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or media
  • No data shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

WatchCTRL uses no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

WatchCTRL does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date above.
